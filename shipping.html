Shipping

Orders are usually processed within available operational timelines. Delivery duration may vary depending on location, courier services, weather conditions, holidays, or other external factors.

Order Confirmation

Customers may receive confirmation through available communication channels after order verification and processing.

Cancellations

Orders may only be cancelled before dispatch. Once an order has been shipped, cancellation requests may not be accepted.

Refund Review

Refund requests, if applicable, may be reviewed in situations involving damaged deliveries or verified order issues. Refund timelines may vary depending on payment methods and operational review.
