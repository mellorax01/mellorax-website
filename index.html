Founder

Built with a young vision, shaped with discipline.

MelloraX is a brand developed under Rana Global Ventures by Gulbahar Rana,, a 16-year-old student entrepreneur from a middle-class village background, currently pursuing Class 12 with PCM and preparing for JEE with the aim of reaching IIT.

His interest in business and entrepreneurship started early, with a simple vision: to make carefully presented honey accessible to Indian households in a premium yet affordable way.

MelloraX is not built to feel mass or ordinary. It is built to bring a calm, refined honey experience — where sourcing, presentation, and customer trust are treated with care.
