Privacy Policy

This page explains how MelloraX, a brand by Rana Global Ventures, handles basic customer information when users visit our website, contact us, place an enquiry, or share feedback.

Information We May Collect

We may collect basic details such as name, phone number, email address, city, delivery address, order details, and feedback shared by the customer.

How We Use Information

Information may be used to process orders, respond to enquiries, manage delivery, collect feedback, improve product experience, and provide customer support.

Data Protection

We aim to handle customer information responsibly and do not sell customer data. Information may be shared only when required for delivery, payment, or support purposes.

Payments

Payment processing, if used, may be handled by third-party payment providers. MelloraX does not store sensitive card or payment details on this website.
